## Overview

For Fall 2025, I worked with fellow classmates to develop a new business line for Allianz, settling in on Sports Season Ticket Insurance.
## Key Features

- **Financial Modeling**: Worked to forecast out cash flow for product in 5 year time horizon, achieving 47 % IRR, and investigated various scenarios to see how they would influence our projections.
- **Marketing Plan**: Personally less involved with this, also Allianz marketing strategy is limited. Worked to develop value propisition to fans, prepare visual touchpoints, and marketing framework.
- **Long Form Presentation**: 50 slide long 20 minute presentation, looking through the entirety of their business segments and delivering succinct presentation on our winning product.
- **Business Strategy**: After analyzing existing strengths, made a point to create a product that aligns with existing offerings, uses existing technologies, and provides the clearest easy win.
