## Miscellaneous Notes
### Album Naming

A huge fan of this album title. For those of us who enjoy finding more underground "good" music, 
this DJ Set disguises their true intentions on the album. Most looking for a DJ Set would be dissapointed hearing this.
Or perhaps its an attempt to redefine what a DJ set looks like. Could a coffee shop playlist be considered a DJ set? If so, then maybe 
this album has a place there. 

### Album Art 
Great album art, not much needed to be said about this honestly. It doesn't reinvent the wheel, but nor does it really need to. The quality music speaks more,
but perhaps its slightly pleasing to the eyes if anything.
