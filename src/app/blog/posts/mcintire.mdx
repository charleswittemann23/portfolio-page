## Short Answer
Mmmm, maybe. There are many determining factors. Like with most things in my life, I regret little, 
but I feel strong that given another chance, I would apply once again.

### Social Aspect
Coming from a computer science background, the commerce social experience was a marked improvement on my social life in class.
Let me paint a picture: 100 person lecture hall, computer screens out, and a general stench of B.O. that fills the air. Now, in McIntire,
one certaintly might struggle with the lack of sunlight; majority of classrooms don't have any windows. But, people talk to one another!
Part of this is do with improved social skills, but another part of this stems from a huge participation grade!

### Career Outcomes

Certainly worth consideration. You will be around many people that are highly driven, connected, and generally easy to work with. 
In any given class, you become aware quickly of highly intelligent people based on how they participate in lecture.
